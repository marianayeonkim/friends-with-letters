Who I am



Having lived in three different places, the United States of America, South Korea, and Hong Kong, I have always been interested in human interactions and relationships.

I would like to connect with kids worldwide, so I created this website to exchange letters or drawings. This is all about nurturing and cultivating relationships with kids on the planet!
